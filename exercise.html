Why did I undertake this project?

I wanted a method of automatically logging any exercise I did, as well as all the relevant information such as the duration, calories burnt and date of exercise. This programme will allow you to keep an upto date record of all the exercises you have done.

How does it work?

The user will type exactly what exercise they have done. For example, '2km run'. The programme will then transfer this information to a google spreadsheet along with: calories burnt, duration of the exercise and the date which the activity was carried out.
